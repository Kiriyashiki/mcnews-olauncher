Ready or not, here comes the Tiny Takeover drop! New versions of baby mobs have stormed Minecraft, causing cuteness overload across biomes and builds. Listen out for heart-melting sounds, craft name tags for your pets, and keep babies forever young with the golden dandelion.<br>

The babies are officially on the loose, so get ready to collect, coo over, and create chaos with these tiny rascals!<br>

**Changelog:**<br>
\* Added new baby sounds for Wolf, Cat, Pig, Horse, and Chicken<br>
\* Added Golden Dandelion<br>
\* Cats have one new sound variant<br>
\* Pigs have two new sound variants<br>
\* Cows have one new sound variant<br>
\* Chickens have one new sound variant<br>
\* The original sounds of each animal are used for the variant called classic<br>
\* Every one of these animals will have a random sound variant assigned to it from the new variants and the original one<br>
\* Added a trumpet instrument for when using a Note Block that is placed on a Copper Block<br>
\* The sound is different based on the oxidation level of the Copper Block<br>
\- Removed Herobrine<br>
