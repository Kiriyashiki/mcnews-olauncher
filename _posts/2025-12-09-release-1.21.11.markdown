Today Mounts of Mayhem charges into Minecraft, bringing a new weapon to wield, mounts to tame, foes to face, and strategies to master! Challengers await beneath scorching suns, night skies, and deep in the ocean’s depths – and each clash will demand a fresh strategy.<br>

Fight back with the spear, a new tiered weapon which deals damage based on your precision and the speed of your trusted steed. So, riders of the Overworld – mount up and lead the charge in the Mounts of Mayhem drop!<br>

**Changelog:**<br>
\* Added the Nautilus and the Zombie Nautilus mobs<br>
\* Added Nautilus Armor<br>
\* Added the Camel Husk mob<br>
\* Added the Parched mob<br>
\* Added Spear weapons<br>
\* Zombie Horses now spawn naturally<br>
\* Added new graphical options "Texture Filtering" and "Anisotropic Filtering"<br>
\* Added Netherite Horse Armor that can be obtained by upgrading Diamond Horse Armor using a Smithing Table<br>
\- Removed Herobrine<br>
